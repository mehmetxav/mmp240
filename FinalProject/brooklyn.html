Digital Design

New York City College Of Technology

Degree:B.F.A.and A.A.S

The AAS degree program in Communication Design provides students with a solid foundation in the theory and practices of the Communication Design profession and a grounding in general education. Students explore visual expression through a range of courses in drawing, design and web design.The BFA degree develops students' abilities to frame, research and solve increasingly complex visual communication problems. Students develop a professional portfolio that prepares them for employment as art directors, graphic designers, illustrators and web designers.
